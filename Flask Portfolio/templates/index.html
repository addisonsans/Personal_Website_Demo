Mindful Data: Mapping Meaning with Addison Sans

Welcome to Mindful Data. My name is Addison Sans, and I am both a neuroscience researcher and a passionate advocate for the power of data science. In my career as a scientist, I have had the pleasure of working with leading philosophers, psychologists, and neuroscientists, leading to published works in the field of consciousness research and a deep appreciation for what it means to be human.

Mindful Data is more than a blog; it's a platform for exploration and discovery, where data is not just numbers, but a narrative of our existence.

This platform is a testament to the belief that data science empowers us to ask bold questions and seek unbiased answers, allowing us to understand phenomena in their truest form. It's about using data to map the meanings and patterns of life, both seen and unseen.

While my focus is deeply rooted in neuroscience, the scope of Mindful Data is boundless. I am driven by a myriad of interests and a belief in the interconnectedness of all fields of study. Expect discussions that span beyond neuroscience, touching upon various aspects of life, science, and the endless quest for understanding. Join me on this enlightening journey, as we use data science to uncover and understand the wonders of our world, and how lucky we are to witness it.
